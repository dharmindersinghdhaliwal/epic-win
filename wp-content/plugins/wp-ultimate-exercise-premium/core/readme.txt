== Description ==

[WP Ultimate Exercise](http://www.wpultimateexercise.com/) is a user friendly plugin for adding exercises to any of your posts and pages. Want to see it all in action? Take a look at our [demo website](http://demo.wpultimateexercise.com) and [video lessons](http://www.wpultimateexercise.com/category/video-lessons/).

This first [video lesson](http://www.wpultimateexercise.com/category/video-lessons/) offers an introduction to WP Ultimate Exercise:

[youtube http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePd2TFCw8aw]

**Your visitors will love WP Ultimate Exercise**. They will be able to adjust the servings, print the exercise, find other exercises using a specific ingredient, share your exercise, ... Try it out today and you'll never want another exercise plugin again!

An overview of the WP Ultimate Exercise features:

*   Use shortcodes or the "Insert Exercise" button to add **multiple exercises to any of your posts and pages**.
*   Ingredients, course types, cuisines and ratings are all **taxonomies** related to your exercise. This means that you can display these in a nice tag cloud widget.
*   Add **photos** to any step of the exercise.
*   Just click the printer icon to **print an image-less version** of the exercise.
*   This plugin is **fully responsive**. That means that your exercises will look good on any device.
*   Uses schema.org/Exercise microdata optimised for **Google Exercise search**.
*   Easily **translatable**, already available in English, Dutch, Swedish and many others
*   Let visitors **adjust the ingredient quantities** for more or less servings
*   Add a **exercise index** to any post or page
*   Structure your ingredients and instructions in **groups** (e.g. icing and cake batter)
*   Visitors can **share your exercises**
*   **Full text search** for your exercises
*   Integration with **BigOven**, **Food Fanatic** and **Chicory** possible

We also have a [WP Ultimate Exercise Premium version](http://www.wpultimateexercise.com/get-the-plugin/) which offers the following features:

*   Create your own unique exercise template with the **Template Editor**
*   Automatically retrieve **Nutritional Information** for your exercises
*   Add your own **custom fields** to your exercises
*   Let your **users submit** exercises
*   User menus with a **shopping list generator**
*   Have your visitors save their **Favorite Exercies**
*   Create **custom tags** for your exercises
*   Advanced **exercise index** features
*   Nice **exercise grid** to show and filter your exercises
*   **Import EasyExercise and EasyExercise Plus** exercises
*   **Import ReciPress** exercises
*   **Import Ziplist** exercises
*   **Import FDX** exercises (Living Cookbook, ...)
*   **Import from and to XML**
*   Custom **ingredient links** (great for affiliate marketing)
*   Let your **users rate your exercises**
*   Advanced usage of **WP categories and tags**
*   Dynamically switch between **Imperial and Metric units**
*   A selection of **Premium Widgets**
*   More **share buttons** available

Feel free to contact us if any features are missing.
You can find more information at [WPUltimateExercise.com](http://www.wpultimateexercise.com/)

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `wp-ultimate-exercise` directory (directory included) to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Add exercises using the menu on the left
